---

The **Employee Account Termination** agent automates offboarding workflows. It runs when a termination is initiated, collects approval, deactivates accounts in the identity provider, and terminates active sessions.

## What it does

- Triggered when an employee termination is initiated (`employee_termination_initiated` event)
- Routes to approvers (e.g., direct manager, HR)
- On approval: deactivates employee in IdP (Okta, Entra, Google Workspace)
- Terminates active sessions
- Updates the ticket with the outcome

## Triggers

**Event** — Runs when `employee_termination_initiated` is raised with `is_termination_request: true`.

## Approval

Requires approval before deactivating accounts. Configure approvers (e.g., direct manager), strategy, and timeout.

## Configuration

- **Approval** — Strategy, approvers, timeout, reminders
- **Identity provider** — Which IdP to deactivate in
- **Session termination** — Behavior for active sessions
