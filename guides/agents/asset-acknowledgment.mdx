title: "Asset Ownership Confirmation Report"
description: "Periodically request asset acknowledgment from employees with MDM-managed devices."
---

The **Asset Ownership Confirmation Report** agent sends asset acknowledgment requests to employees who have MDM-managed devices. It runs on a schedule and collects acknowledgments for compliance and inventory accuracy.

## What it does

- Targets employees with assigned devices (or specific employee IDs)
- Sends asset acknowledgment requests (via portal or configured channel)
- Tracks responses and sends reminders as configured
- Creates tickets for non-responders or escalations

## Triggers

**Schedule** — Runs at configured frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.) via cron.

## Approval

No approval required for sending requests. Approval workflows can be configured for escalation or follow-up.

## Configuration

- **Run Schedule** — Frequency, day, time, timezone
- **Notification** — Timeout, reminder intervals, max reminders
- **Ticket** — Desk, assignee, priority for escalation tickets
- **Target scope** — All employees with assets, or specific employee IDs
