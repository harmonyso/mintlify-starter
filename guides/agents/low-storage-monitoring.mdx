---
title: "Low Storage Device Monitoring"
description: "Automated monitoring for devices with critically low storage capacity."
---

The **Low Storage Device Monitoring** agent finds devices with critically low free storage and sends alerts or creates tickets so IT can remediate before users are blocked.

## What it does

- Queries device storage from MDM integrations
- Compares free space against a configurable threshold (e.g., % or GB)
- Sends alerts or creates tickets when devices fall below the threshold
- Can target specific devices or an entire scope

## Triggers

**Schedule** — Runs at configured frequency (e.g., daily, weekly) via cron.

## Approval

No approval required. The agent monitors and alerts; remediation is handled manually or by other workflows.

## Configuration

- **Run Schedule** — Frequency, day, time, timezone
- **Storage threshold** — Percentage (e.g., under 10%) or absolute amount (e.g., under 5 GB)
- **Scope** — All devices or filtered subset
- **Alert/Ticket** — Desk, assignee, priority for created tickets
